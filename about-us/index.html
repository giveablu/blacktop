About BlackTop Diesel & Tire LLC

BlackTop Diesel & Tire LLC provides professional mobile mechanical service for heavy-duty trucks, forklifts, off-road equipment, and industrial machinery throughout Jacksonville, Florida. Built on three generations of diesel experience, our focus is accurate diagnostics, dependable repairs, and minimizing costly downtime for our clients.

We specialize in electrical troubleshooting, hydraulic systems, brake repairs, starting and charging systems, preventive maintenance, and on-site equipment service. Whether it’s a fleet truck, warehouse forklift, construction equipment, or industrial air compressor, our goal is to identify the root cause of the issue and repair it correctly the first time.

Modern equipment is complex. Instead of guessing or replacing unnecessary parts, we use a diagnostic-first approach to deliver reliable solutions that keep your operation running safely and efficiently.

We are committed to clear communication, professional workmanship, and long-term business relationships built on trust and consistency.
